# Code of Conduct

heyfood contributors are expected to create a respectful, technically rigorous,
and welcoming environment.

## Expected behavior

- Treat contributors and users with patience and respect.
- Critique ideas and implementations without attacking people.
- Make room for different backgrounds, dietary practices, disabilities,
  cultures, and levels of technical experience.
- Handle food, body, disability, allergy, and medical topics without stigma or
  moral judgment.
- Protect personal information and obtain consent before sharing another
  person’s experiences or dietary needs.
- Accept maintainer decisions made to protect users, compatibility, security,
  or the project’s conservative food-safety language.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption,
doxing, deliberate disclosure of private information, and knowingly unsafe or
deceptive technical guidance are not acceptable. Retaliation against someone
who raises a good-faith concern is also prohibited.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `hi@frntr.ai` with the subject
`[heyfood conduct]`. Do not use a public issue. Include only the information
needed to understand the concern and avoid forwarding private health or account
data.

FRNTR, LLC maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict
participation, issue a warning, or impose a temporary or permanent ban. Reports
will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible, with conflicts of
interest routed away from the involved maintainer when another qualified
reviewer is available.

This policy applies in project repositories, community spaces, and private
communications made in an official project role.
